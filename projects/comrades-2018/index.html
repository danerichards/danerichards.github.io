Running Comrades 2018 for Wildlands Trust

February 1, 2018 by Dane

The Story

Friends & Family, I would like to ask you for a favour:

On the 4th of November 2017, after having run two previous marathons, I finally ran a time that qualified me for a spot in the 2 Oceans Ultra Marathon 2018, and the Comrades Marathon 2018.

For the next 4 months - February, March, April and May - a massive volume of kilometers will be run in order to prepare for running the 89km from Pietermaritzburg down to Durban.

In a somewhat mutually beneficial way, I’ve committed myself to raise funds for charity.

On my side this will give me a modicum of commitment in order to complete all the training required, and additionally, a spot into the ‘C’ starting batch, which gives me a precious extra 7 minutes over my current starting batch of ‘G’ - very useful in order to finish under 12 hours :-)

On the side of the charity, they get to benefit from all the generous donations received.
